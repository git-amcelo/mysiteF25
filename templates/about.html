Our Mission & History

Founded by a small group of enthusiastic engineers, our mission is to foster technical excellence, inspire creativity, and cultivate leadership skills among our members. We believe that technology can change the world, and we provide our members with the playground to do exactly that.

Over the years, our club has achieved major milestones, including winning top honors at regional coding competitions and helping dozens of members secure internships at prominent tech companies.

Club Timeline & Milestones

  1. September 2023: The club is officially founded with 15 initial members.
  2. January 2024: Hosted our very first internal 24-hour Hackathon.
  3. September 2024: Membership expanded to over 100 students across various disciplines.
  4. March 2025: Won 1st place at the National University Innovation Challenge.
